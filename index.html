Steps Cafe

A cute peaceful cafe with vegan options!

Address:

29/8 Ekamai Soi 10, Sukhumvit 63, Bangkok 10110 Thailand

What I like about it

It's a cafe that helps neurodivergent people learn how to own and operate a cafe. Along with other education that they provide to anyone wanting to own or work here. I think this is amazing, and it shows a lot of kindness and care!
